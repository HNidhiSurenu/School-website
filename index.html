Campus Life

School Building

The school building was established in the year 2003

The building comprises of about four floors. There are two main blocks: one for classes 3rd-8th and the other one is the admin block for classes from 9th-12th and the cambridge sections.



School Ground

The school has a huge playground for the students' use.

The school also pays special attention to the extra curricular education of children. The school encourages students to play various sports like football, cricket, badminton, basketball, athletics, swimming,etc and also provides students with appropriate equipment, opportunities and guidance. Other vital facilities like proper courts and grounds are also provided. There are coaches specialized in each sport who guide, explain rules and help the students understand and play the sport.



School Library

This is the school library

There are two magnificent libraries in the school; one in the admin block and one in the main block. From fiction to biographies to textbooks, the two libraries have it all. There are tables and chairs where the students can comfortably read their choice of books. The students are also allowed to borrow books.
